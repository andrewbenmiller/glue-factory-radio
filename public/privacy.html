Privacy Policy

Glue Factory Radio

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Glue Factory Radio is a free internet radio app. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or login required
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No cookies or device identifiers
  • No location data
  • No access to contacts, camera, microphone, or other device sensors

Network Requests

The app connects to Glue Factory Radio servers solely to:

  • Stream live audio
  • Load show and episode metadata (titles, descriptions, track listings)
  • Load page content (e.g., the About page)

These requests do not include any personal or device-identifying information.

Caching

The app caches audio and images locally on your device to improve performance. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above.
